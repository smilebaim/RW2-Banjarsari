# **App Name**: Banjarsari Connect

## Core Features:

- Community News & Announcements: A centralized feed to display the latest announcements and news from RW management, keeping residents informed.
- RW Management Directory: A dedicated section showcasing profiles, roles, and contact information for the RW 2 Banjarsari management team.
- Resident Feedback & Report Form: An intuitive form for residents to submit aspirations, general feedback, or report specific issues directly to the RW administration.
- Important Contacts List: A clear and easily searchable list of essential local contacts, such as emergency services, local government offices, and community leaders.
- AI News Summarization Tool: An AI tool that automatically processes longer news articles and announcements, providing concise summaries to help residents quickly grasp key information.
- AI Feedback Analysis Tool: An AI tool to assist RW management by analyzing submitted resident feedback and reports, identifying common themes, sentiments, and urgent issues for prioritization.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen light color scheme is designed for a sense of freshness and community. The primary color is a grounded forest green (#359644), symbolizing growth and connection. The background uses a heavily desaturated variant of the primary green (#EEF6EF) to provide a clean and airy canvas, appearing almost white. A vibrant yellow (#F5E630) is used sparingly as an accent to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to ensure excellent readability and a modern, clean, and objective aesthetic that is perfect for an informative community portal.
- Use line-art style icons that are easily recognizable and reinforce the clean, informative design, focusing on community-related visuals (e.g., chat bubbles for feedback, house for profile).
- Adopt a clean, grid-based layout for consistent content organization. Prioritize ample whitespace to enhance readability and ensure a user-friendly, uncluttered interface.
- Implement subtle transition animations for content loading and navigation, providing smooth visual feedback without being distracting. Use a gentle fade-in for new information appearing in the news feed.
